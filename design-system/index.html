01 · Foundation

Brand Identity

The Bold logo is a two-part SVG lockup: a three-stripe mark (cyan → blue → purple, staggered diagonally) and the dark wordmark "BOLD". The mark represents motion and momentum. At small sizes (mobile nav), the wordmark is hidden and only the mark is shown. The wordmark uses #140D26 on light backgrounds and switches to #fff on dark.

Three timing tiers. Always use ease or cubic-bezier — never linear for UI. Animations signal state, not decoration.
